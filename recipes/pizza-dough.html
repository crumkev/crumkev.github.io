Makes 3 personal size pizza crusts

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - approx. 2 ¼ cups
  • Dry active yeast - 1 tbsp
  • Olive oil - 1 tsp
  • Salt - ¼ tsp
  • Water (lukewarm) - 11 oz

Cookware

  • Large bowl - 1
  • Cookie sheet - 1
  • Dough whisk - 1
  • Pastry brush - 1
  • Graduated measuring cup - 1
  • Measuring cups/spoons - ¼ cup, 1 tbsp, 1 tsp, ¼ tsp
  • Plastic food wrap - enough to cover cookie sheet

Instructions

  1. Add 11 oz water, 1 tbsp yeast, and ¼ tsp salt to the large bowl. Stir until dissolved.
  2. Add ¾ cups of all purpose flour to the large bowl. Whisk until incorporated.
  3. Add 1 tsp olive oil to the large bowl. Whisk until incorporated.
  4. Add ¾ cups of all purpose flour to the large bowl. Whisk until incorporated.
  5. Add ¼ cups of all purpose flour to the large bowl. Whisk until incorporated. Repeat until dough is desired texture.
  6. Transfer dough to a flat, floured surface. Knead approx. 10 times.
  7. Split dough into 3 similarly sized pieces.
  8. Take 1 piece of dough, cloak, shape into a ball, and place on oiled cookie sheet. Repeat with 2 other pieces.
  9. Cover the cookie sheet in plastic food wrap.
  10. Allow to rise for approx. 2 hours.
