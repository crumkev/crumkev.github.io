  • Limoncello Spritz

Makes 1 drink

Ingredients

  • Limoncello - 2 parts
  • Prosecco - 3 parts
  • Seltzer Water - 1 part
  • Basil leaves - as desired

Cookware

  • jigger or shot glass - 1

Instructions

  1. Add limoncello, prosecco, and seltzer to glass.
  2. Garnish with basil leaves.
