  • Balsamic Reduction

Makes ½ cup

Ingredients

  • Balsamic vinegar - 1 cup

Cookware

  • Small pot - 1

Instructions

  1. Add balsamic vinegar to small pot.
  2. Simmer on low heat.
  3. Reduction is ready when it is thick enough to coat a spoon (approx. 10 minutes).
