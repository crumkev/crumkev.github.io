  • Simple Margarita

Makes 1 drink

Ingredients

  • Tequila - 3 oz
  • Triple Sec - 2 oz
  • Lime juice - 1 oz (approx. 1 lime)
  • Ice - as desired

Cookware

  • Jigger - 1

Instructions

  1. Measure each ingredient with jigger and add to glass.
  2. Stir.
  3. Add ice.
