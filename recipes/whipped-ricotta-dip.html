  • Whipped Ricotta Dip

Makes as much as desired

Ingredients

  • Ricotta - as desired
  • Honey - as desired
  • Pistachios - as desired
  • Extra virgin olive oil - as desired

Cookware

  • Mixing bowl - 1
  • Serving dish - 1
  • Egg beater - 1

Instructions

  1. Add ricotta to mixing bowl.
  2. Use egg beater on ricotta until desired consistency is reached (approx. 1 minute).
  3. Transfer whipped ricotta to serving dish.
  4. Top with honey, extra virgin olive oil, and pistachios.
