  • Sofrito Rice

Makes 2 cups of rice

Ingredients

  • Jasmine rice - 1 cup
  • Sofrito - 1 tbsp
  • Veggie bouillon - 2 tsp
  • Minced garlic - 1 tsp
  • Canola oil - 2 tsp
  • Water - 2 cups

Cookware

  • Medium saucepan - 1

Instructions

  1. Add canola oil to the medium saucepan. Heat on medium-low.
  2. Add minced garlic. Saute until starting to brown.
  3. Add jasmine rice. Stir and saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Add sofrito. Stir and saute for 2 minutes.
  5. Add water and veggie bouillon. Stir to dissolve.
  6. Cover and increase heat to high. Bring to a simmer.
  7. Once simmering reduce heat to maintain. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Fluff rice, cover and let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
