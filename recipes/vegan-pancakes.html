Makes 4 medium pancakes

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - 1 ½ cups
  • Baking powder - ½ tbsp
  • Applesauce - 2 tbsp
  • Almond milk - 1 cup
  • Vanilla extract - ½ tsp
  • Water - ⅓ cups
  • Cooking spray - as needed

Cookware

  • Large skillet - 1
  • Medium mixing bowl - 1
  • Dough whisk - 1

Instructions

  1. Heat large skillet on medium heat.
  2. Add all ingredients in medium mixing bowl. Mix with dough whisk.
  3. Spray cooking oil onto skillet. Pour batter onto skillet.
  4. Flip pancake when edges start to dry and center starts to bubble.
  5. Cook for additional 30 seconds.
  6. Repeat until all batter is gone.
