  • Air Fried Tofu

Makes 1 block of tofu

Ingredients

  • Extra firm tofu - 1 block
  • Cornstarch - 2½ tbsp
  • Nutritional yeast - 2½ tbsp
  • Soy sauce - 2½ tbsp
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic powder - 1 tsp
  • Paprika - 1 tsp

Cookware

  • Air frier - 1
  • Medium bowl - 1

Instructions

  1. Cut tofu into desired size chunks.
  2. Mix cornstarch, nutritional yeast, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, and paprika in medium bowl.
  3. Coat tofu chunks in spiced batter.
  4. Air fry tofu chunks at 400°F for 10 minutes.
  5. Shake tofu chunks and air fry for another 5 minutes at 400°F.
