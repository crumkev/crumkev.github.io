Makes 2 waffles

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Baking powder - 2 tsp
  • Applesauce - ¼ cups
  • Almond milk - 1½ cups
  • Vanilla - ¼ tsp

Cookware

  • Waffle iron - 1
  • Medium bowl - 1

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to medium bowl.
  2. Mix and let rest for 10 minutes.
  3. While batter is resting, heat waffle iron.
  4. Add batter to waffle iron and cook.
