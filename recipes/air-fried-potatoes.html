  • Air Fried Potatoes

Makes 1½ lbs of potatoes

Ingredients

  • Baby potatoes - 1½ lbs
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder - ½ tsp
  • Olive oil - to coat

Cookware

  • Air frier - 1
  • Medium bowl - 1

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes in half and put in medium bowl.
  2. Sprinkle paprika, garlic powder, and olive oil on top of potatoes.
  3. Coat potatoes in spices.
  4. Place potatoes in a single layer in the air fryer.
  5. Air fry potatoes at 380°F for 10 minutes.
  6. Shake potatoes air fry for another 10 minutes at 380°F.
