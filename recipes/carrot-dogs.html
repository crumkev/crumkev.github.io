6 carrot dogs

Ingredients

  • Carrots - 6
  • Hot dog buns - 6
  • Apple cider vinegar - ¼ cup
  • Soy sauce - 3 tbsp
  • Maple syrup - 1½ tbsp
  • Liquid smoke - 1 tbsp
  • Smoked paprika - 1 tsp
  • Garlic - 1 clove
  • Black pepper - ¼ tsp
  • Veggie bouillon - 2 tsp
  • Water - 2 cups

Cookware

  • Large pot - 1
  • Marinade dish - 1

Instructions

  1. Peel and boil carrots in large pot for 10 minutes.
  2. While carrots are boiling, mix apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, maple syrup, liquid smoke, garlic, black pepper, veggie bouillon, and water in marinade dish.
  3. When carrots are tender, transfer to marinade dish, and marinade for at least 15 minutes.
  4. Grill carrots for 10 minutes, flipping once.
  5. Grill hot dog buns for 1 minute.
